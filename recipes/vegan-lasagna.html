Vegan Lasagna

Yummy vegan lasagna that doesn't leave out the meat or cheese.

You'll love this vegan recipe and won't be hurting animals by making it.

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Beyond Meat crumble
  • Daiya mozzarella shreds
  • Tomato sauce
  1. Cook lasagna noodles
  2. Add to bottom of baking dish
  3. Mix Beyond Meat crumble and tomato sauce
  4. Layer tomato sauce and Beyond Meat crumble mix
  5. Layer of Daiya shreds
  6. Layer of noodles
  7. Continue layering until you run out of ingredients
  8. Bake at 400 for 20 minutes
