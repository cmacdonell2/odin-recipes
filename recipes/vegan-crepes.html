Vegan Crepes

Yummy vegan crepes that only have four ingredients.

You'll love this vegan recipe and won't be hurting animals by making it.

  • Flour
  • Ground flax seeds
  • Almond milk
  • Pinch of salt
  1. Mix 3 tablespoons of water with 1 tablespoon of flax meal
  2. Add 125g of flour to mixing bowl
  3. Add pinch of salt to flour
  4. Whisk dry ingredients
  5. Add 250ml of almond milk and flax egg to mixing bowl
  6. Whisk to combine
  7. Fry 1/4 to 1/3 cup at a time
